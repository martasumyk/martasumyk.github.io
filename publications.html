GBEM-UA: Gender Bias Evaluation and Mitigation for Ukrainian Large Language Models

Authors: Mykhailo Buleshnyi, Maksym Buleshnyi, Marta Sumyk, Nazarii Drushchak

UNLP 2025, co-located with ACL 2025

In a team of three, developed a benchmark for measuring gender bias in the hiring problem within the Ukrainian language. Researched four debiasing methods: prompting, soft debiasing, hard debiasing, and LoRA fine-tuning.

Screen2AX: Vision-Based Approach for Automatic macOS Accessibility Generation

Authors: Viktor Muryn, Marta Sumyk, Mariya Hirna, Sofiya Garkot, Maksym Shamrai

Developed a vision-only pipeline for reconstructing accessibility trees of macOS applications. The approach outperformed the current screen-grounding baseline.
