Marta Sumyk

I am interested in artificial intelligence, reinforcement learning, computer use agents, vision-language models, and accessibility evaluation.

My current research focuses on using autonomous feedback from vision-language models to evaluate and improve computer use agents. I am especially interested in how AI systems can interact with graphical user interfaces, receive feedback, and improve through reinforcement learning.

Research Interests

  • Vision-Language Models
  • Human-Centered AI
  • Digital Accessibility

Selected Projects

Reinforcement Learning with Autonomous Feedback for Computer Use Agents

Vision-Based Judge for Autonomous Task Completion
